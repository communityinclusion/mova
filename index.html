Employment Options is a free national service available to anyone impacted by the 2013 Boston Marathon bombing. We will assist you in considering your career choices and getting you back in the workforce or help you retain your existing job.

If you are located outside of Massachusetts we will work with you via phone, email, videoconference, and the Internet to provide ongoing guidance and support and help you navigate employment services in your area.

Employment Options provides an array of services that are customized to meet your individual needs. These services include:

  • Working one-on-one with a trained vocational specialist
  • Career guidance and support as you consider entering employment or to help you retain your current job
  • Career exploration activities to determine good job matches based on your skills, interests, and abilities
  • Advice and consultation on job search techniques, including networking, online applications, and personality tests
  • Access to other employment services, such as vocational rehabilitation
  • Help identifying and applying to jobs that fit your career goals
  • Finding community resources to address other employment-related needs, such as transportation
  • Assistance with reasonable accommodations and/ or assistive technology
